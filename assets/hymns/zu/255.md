## Ngikudingile, Jesu.

Ngikudingile Jesu
Loku ngingo mpofu
Nging’ umuntu’ ozulayo
Angi nay’ imfuyo
Ngidinga Wena Jesu
Ngibambe umgudu
Welus’ inyawo zami
Nayo inhliziyo

Ngidinga Yen’ uJesu
Njalo anginake
Ngize ngilwe nes’lingo
Engelekelela
Ngiding’ uMoy’ oNgcwele
Nami ngibe naYe
Ngifundiswe ngoJesu
Onge yam’ indlela

O Jesu, oligugu!
Ngoza nini kuWe
Ngihlotshiswe ngobuhle
Ngibe nobukhosi
Kuthi naba sindiswa
Njalo ngeneliswe
Ngicule ngingaphezi
Ngibusa neNkosi, Amen!
