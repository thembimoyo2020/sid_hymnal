## Nkosi Sihlangene

Nkosi sihlangene
Kuyo indlu yaKho;
Yiza nawe, sesingene,
Ebusweni baKho.

Intliziyo yethu
Iyakugulela;
Nawo umphefumlo wethu
Uyakukhangela.

Ubuncoko baKho,
Bawo, masibuve;
Lakutyhilwa izwi laKho,
Nawe masikuve.

Mawusilungise
Thina bantu baKho;
Wakufika, singenise
Ezulwini laKho.
