## Nkosi Yam

Nkosi yam, ubundithanda,
Ekulahlekeni kwam,
Nkosi yam, waman' ulanda
Futhi emkhondweni wam,
Ndafunyanwa, Nkosi yam.

Nkosi yam, ilizwi laKho
Lingukhanyiseli wam.
Nkosi yam, ufefe lwaKho
Lusisonwabiso sam.
Ndinovuyo, Nkosi.

Nkosi yam, u-Moya waKho
Uyandingcwalisa nam.
Nkosi yam, izulu laKho
Lona lilikhaya lam.
Mandingene, Nkosi yam.
