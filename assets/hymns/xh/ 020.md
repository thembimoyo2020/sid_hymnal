## Vuka, Moya Wam

Vuka, khuthala moya wam,
\tQhubela phambili;
Batsho njalo ezulwini,
\tNaso isitsaba.

Leli lizwi lika-Thixo
\tElikubizayo;
Nguye onika umvuzo
\tKuw' okhutheleyo.

Maninzi ke amangqina
\tAkubonelayo;
Yeka indlela zalapha,
\tTsalela phezulu.

Singeniswe ngu-msindisi
\tWazo zonk' izizwe;
Sobeka ezinyaweni
\tLowo mvuzo wethu.
