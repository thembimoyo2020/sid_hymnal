## Isikolo Se-Sabatha

Isikolo se-Sabatha,
Siya thandeka kum;
Intliziyo iya vuya
Ngomhla we-Sabatha.

Chorus
Sabatha . . . emnandi . . . 
Sabatha . . . emnandi . . .
Intliziyo iya vuya

Apha ndaqala ndabona
Indlela yempilo;Apha ndacinga okuhle,
Ndazuza ikhaya.

Wayemi khona u-Yesu,
Wandibiza yena,
Ukuba ndimkhethe Yena
Ne-Sabatha yaKhe.
