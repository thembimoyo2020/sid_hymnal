## Ungandidluli

Ungandidluli, Msindisi,
Yiv' amazwi am;
Apho ubabiz' abanye,
Mawundive nam

Chorus
Nkosi, Nkosi, yiv' amazwi am,
Apho ubabiz' abanye, Mawundive nam.

Kweso sihlalo senceba,
Sendikhona nam;
Ndiguqile, ndiyhakhala,
Yiva Nkosi yam.

Sekukade ndilindile,
Ndiyathemba nam;
'Zundibophe, ndaphukile,
Ndisindise nam.

Ndifikile emthonjeni,
Nokuphila kwam;
Ezulwini nasezweni
Kukho konk' okwam.
