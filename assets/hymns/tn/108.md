## Jeso Modisa Yo Lorato Wa Go Bitsa

Jeso, Modisa Yo Lorato Wa Go Bitsa
Go tsena polokegong Mo go boikhutso;
Tla nonofong ya bonna, Tla mosong wa bokau,
Tsena mo polokegong, Tseleng boammaaruri.

Chorus
Loratong ka bonolo Oa bitsa;
Tla motimedi, tla kwano go Nna,
Ke mmona A eme ka boitshoko,
Jeso Modisa wa me.

Jeso Modisa Yo O'rato O Go Swetse,
Mme janong wa go bitsa, Motimedi tla go Nna;
Akofa go diphatsa, Go bitsa Modisa,
Tsena mo polokegong, Tsena boikhutsong.

Tiego Ke Bomatla, Diphiri Di Tletse,
Di batla letimela, Go bol'a dikwana;
Jeso Modisa yo O Rato Oa go bitsa,
Go tsena polokegong Mo go leng boikhutso.
